Types of Home Loan Interest Rate

  • Fixed Rate Loans: Interest rate remains constant throughout the loan tenure.
  • Floating Rate Loans: Interest rate varies with market conditions.
  • Hybrid Loans: Fixed for a certain period, then becomes floating.

4 Factors Determining Interest Rates of Home Loan

  1. Credit Score: A higher score means better loan terms.
  2. Home Loan Type: Type of loan affects rate offered.
  3. Occupation Profile: Stable income sources get better rates.
  4. Type of Interest Rate: Fixed vs Floating impacts EMI and risk.

FAQs on Home Loan Interest Rate

Q: What is the current home loan interest rate?
A: Starting from 7.35% p.a. based on credit profile and lender.

Q: Which bank offers the best rate?
A: Bank of India, Central Bank, and Bank of Maharashtra offer rates from 7.35% p.a.

Q: Does RBI repo rate affect my interest?
A: Yes, especially for floating rate home loans linked to external benchmarks like RLLR.

Q: Fixed or Floating – which is better?
A: Floating is lower but fluctuates. Fixed gives EMI predictability but may be higher.
