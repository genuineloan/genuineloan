Types of Home Loans

  • Fixed Rate Loan: Interest rate remains constant.
  • Floating Rate Loan: Interest rate changes as per market trends.
  • Balance Transfer: Switch existing loan to another bank with lower rates.

Factors Affecting Home Loan Rates

  • Credit score of the borrower.
  • Loan amount and tenure.
  • Type of interest rate chosen (fixed/floating).
  • Property type and location.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

Q1: What is the minimum CIBIL score required?
A: Most banks require a minimum score of 650–700.

Q2: Can I prepay my home loan?
A: Yes, most banks allow prepayment without penalty on floating rate loans.

Q3: Are women borrowers offered concessional rates?
A: Yes, many banks offer 0.05% lower rates for women applicants.
